You are opening an Excalibur Discovery session for a B2B SaaS product team.

Raw input ({{inputType}}):

{{input}}

Restate the input as a problem statement in at most three sentences:
who has the problem, what they cannot do today, and why it matters now.
Do not propose solutions. Then recommend which question pack to run
(product-discovery or agent-readiness) and say which questions you expect
to be hardest to answer, so the team can prepare evidence in advance.
